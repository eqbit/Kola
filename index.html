Lumbovka
Kachkovka
Stretching for 85 km, the river hosts our central camp, in the middle of the salmon accessible stretch at 15 km from the delta. Lumbovka offers a lot of angling space, with pools averaging 30 m in width and accessible walking landscape, making it suitable for different skill levels. Geographic location, between White and Barents seas, offers both summer and fall salmon and sea trout fishing. In the only surveillance fishing trip in the last 8 years, we have caught samples up to 17 kg.
